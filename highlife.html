Highlife

Discover the upbeat and soulful sounds of Highlife, a genre that originated in West Africa, combining indigenous rhythms with Western instruments.

Overview

Highlife music originated in Ghana in the early 20th century, blending traditional African rhythms with Western instruments like the guitar and trumpet. It became popular in several West African countries.

Notable Artists

  • E.T. Mensah - One of the most iconic figures in Highlife music.
  • Osibisa - A pioneering Highlife band that gained international fame.
  • Amakye Dede - A famous Ghanaian Highlife musician.

Popular Songs

  • Odo Pa - Amakye Dede
  • Yaa Amponsah - E.T. Mensah
  • Music for You - Osibisa
