Afrobeat

Explore the energetic and rhythmic sounds of Afrobeat, a fusion of traditional African rhythms with jazz, highlife, and funk.

Overview

Afrobeat originated in Nigeria in the late 1960s, famously pioneered by Fela Kuti. It blends traditional Nigerian music with funk, jazz, and highlife, creating an infectious and danceable sound that has influenced global music.

Notable Artists

  • Fela Kuti - Known as the pioneer of Afrobeat.
  • Seun Kuti - Fela's son and a prominent figure in Afrobeat.
  • Antibalas - A Brooklyn-based Afrobeat band.

Popular Songs

  • Water No Get Enemy - Fela Kuti
  • Zombie - Fela Kuti
  • Shuffering and Shmiling - Fela Kuti
